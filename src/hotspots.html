Hotspot Creation

This will allow you to create hotspots on the video you uploaded earlier. To create a hotspot click on any part of the video and give it a name. You will be able to view the coordinates for the hotspots as well. Next, choose the video to link the hotspot to. Continue by saving the hotspot and selecting the next video.
